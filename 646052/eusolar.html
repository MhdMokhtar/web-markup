Renewable energy statistics:

The use of renewable energy has many potential benefits, including a reduction in greenhouse gas emissions, the diversification of energy supplies, and a reduced dependency on fossil fuel markets (in particular, oil and gas). The growth of renewable energy sources may also stimulate employment in the EU, through the creation of jobs in new 'green' technologies.
